Meet Our AI Van

AutoBotic

The Ai van is outfitted with self-driving system which will drive up to 200 miles per charge and works on a schedule created for it. This Van Takes 30 min to charge completely. This Van contains a Solar panel system to charge the car while it is on the way. It has automatic Sanitation Spray System Which keeps COVID-19 virus far from your parcels. This Van Has A great management system which manages parcel automatically. This has been Designed keeping narrow roads in mind .

The AI van comprises of 4 HD cameras, one that faces forward, two that face out to each side, and one driver facing camera. Utilising AI technology and machine learning, the camera can detect obstacles, calculate speed and distance for other road users and also recognise road signs. The driver facing camera can also recognise if the driver is getting distracted.

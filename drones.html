Meet Our Drone

FB3 Cargo Drone

The FB3 cargo drone designed by Flying Basket is an UAV that carries heavy payloads up to 100kg to mountainous areas and other remote areas that are difficult to access with conventional motorized vehicles. Autopilot allows the cargo drone to fly with a high level of safety standards.

The delivery aircraft has been designed with an architecture consisting of 8 rotors, which makes it a safe and reliable UAV

Drone Ports

There is going to be one drone port in each state. Here The Drones Will Land And Deliver Parcels. The center point would have a big dish band antenna that will help drone communicate to prevent accidents. Each Drone Port Will Have 7 drones with it.
